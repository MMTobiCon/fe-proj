About Us

Our cafe, we believe that great coffee is more than just a beverage; it's an experience. in the heart of City of Waterloo, our coffee shop is more than just a place to grab your cup;it is a community hub where people come together to savor delicious brews,indulge in treats, and connect with one another in a warm and inviting atmosphere.

Our Coffee

At Conestoga Condor Coffee, we take pride in every cup we serve. Our skilled baristas are dedicated to the art of coffee making, carefully preparing each drink to perfection. Whether you're craving a classic espresso, a creamy latte, or a refreshing cold brew, you can trust that every sip will be a delight to the senses. We source our beans from sustainable and ethical sources, ensuring that every cup supports the hard work of coffee farmers and contributes to a more equitable and sustainable coffee industry.

Our Space

Step into Conestoga Condor Caffee and you'll immediately feel at home. Our cozy and inviting is the perfect place to unwind, catch up with friends, or simply enjoy a moment of solitude your favorite brew. With comfortable seating, free Wi-Fi, and a welcoming ambiance, we invite you to make yourself at home and stay awhile.

Community

At Conestoga Condor Coffee, we believe in the power of community. That's why we're committed to giving back to the neighborhoods we serve. Whether it's hosting local events, supporting charitable causes, or simply providing a welcoming space for people to come together, we're proud to be a positive force in our community.

Visit Us

We invite you to join us at Conestoga Condor Caffe and experience the magic for yourself. Whether you're a seasoned coffee aficionado or a casual caffeine enthusiast, you'll find something to love at our shop. So come on in, pull up a chair, and let's share a cup of something wonderful together.
